🛠️ Quick Troubleshooting

🔄

Update the app

Go to the App Store → Updates → LittleMinds and install the latest version.

📶

Check your connection

The app works offline, but syncing features need a stable internet connection.

🔁

Restart the app

Swipe the app away from the app switcher and reopen it fresh.

📱

Restart your device

A quick reboot fixes most performance and loading issues on any device.
