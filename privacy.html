🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

LittleMinds Learning ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to provide the service:

  • Parent account information: Name, email address, and password (hashed and stored securely).
  • Learning progress data: Stars earned, tasks completed, and modules finished — stored locally on your device.

We do not collect any personal information from children directly.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

LittleMinds Learning is designed for children under 13, used under parent or guardian supervision. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All account creation is done by parents or guardians only.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the learning app
  • To allow parents to monitor their child's progress
  • To respond to support enquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Learning progress is stored locally on your device. Parent account credentials are stored securely. We do not currently use cloud-based servers to store your personal data.

5. Advertising

LittleMinds Learning contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not use advertising networks or tracking tools.

6. In-App Purchases

The app contains no in-app purchases. All content is available at no additional cost.

7. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or social media services within the app.

8. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email us at support@hounviotech.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

📧 support@hounviotech.com
